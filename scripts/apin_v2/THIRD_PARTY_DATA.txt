APIN Analytics tab — third-party libraries & data (9.N.T31)
============================================================

Vendored, lazy-loaded ONLY on the per-key Analytics tab.

globe.gl  v2.46.1                 — globe.gl.min.js
    3D globe visualization (bundles three.js).
    License: MIT. (c) Vasco Asturiano. https://github.com/vasturiano/globe.gl

Natural Earth (admin-0, admin-1)  — geo_world_countries.json,
                                    geo_world_states.json
    Country + state/province boundaries.
    License: Public Domain (no rights reserved). https://www.naturalearthdata.com
    Source vectors: github.com/nvkelso/natural-earth-vector
    Property set trimmed + coordinates rounded to 4 dp for size.

geoBoundaries (IND ADM2)          — geo_district_IN.json
    India district boundaries (simplified).
    License: CC BY 4.0 — ATTRIBUTION REQUIRED.
    Runfola, D. et al. (2020) geoBoundaries: A global database of political
    administrative boundaries. PLoS ONE 15(4): e0231866.
    https://www.geoboundaries.org  (gbOpen, IND ADM2_simplified)
    Property set trimmed + coordinates rounded to 4 dp for size.
    NOTE: attribution is surfaced in-app on the Analytics geographic-origin
    widget (map credit line).

Crop meshes (3D inference field)
    Decided in 9.N.T31e: stylized plants are generated procedurally in
    three.js with an ink/toon shader (no external mesh dependency). If CC0
    Quaternius meshes (https://quaternius.com, CC0) are vendored instead,
    list them here — CC0 needs no attribution but we credit anyway.
